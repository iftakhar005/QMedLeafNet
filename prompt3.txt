1.1 OVERALL PIPELINE STRUCTURE

The pipeline consists of THREE MAIN VERTICAL SECTIONS (left to right):

SECTION A: INPUT & DATA FOUNDATION (Left Column, Blue Tones)
├─ Raw Images (1440×1080 PNG/JPG)
│  └─ 25,962 total images
│     ├─ 1,981 original images
│     └─ 23,981 augmented images
├─ 7 Species
├─ 9 Health Conditions
└─ 63 Total Classes (7 × 9)

SECTION B: PROCESSING & FEATURE EXTRACTION (Middle Column, Purple/Indigo Tones)
├─ Preprocessing Pipeline (5 Steps)
├─ Backbone Feature Extractor (MobileNetV3-Small)
├─ Feature Vectors (576-dimensional)
└─ 4 Classical Machine Learning Heads

SECTION C: OUTPUT & RESULTS (Right Column, Green Tones)
├─ Classification Predictions
├─ Performance Metrics
├─ Cross-Fold Results
└─ Benchmarks & Comparisons
