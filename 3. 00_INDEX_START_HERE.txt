MANUAL STEPS (You Must Do These - Cannot Be Automated):

1. DOWNLOAD DATASET (Day 1)
   - Go to: https://data.mendeley.com/datasets/23tnz64w5m/1
   - Download all files, accept license, extract to data/raw/
   - This cannot be automated (Mendeley requires human interaction)

2. EXAMINE FILENAME PATTERNS (Day 6)
   - Run: find data/raw -type f -name "*.jpg" | head -20
   - Show Claude the first 20 filenames
   - Claude will adjust extraction script based on actual filenames

3. VERIFY LABEL ONTOLOGY (Day 7 - CRITICAL GATE)
   - Review label_ontology.json output
   - Confirm all 7 species and all conditions present
   - Approve or request changes (cannot proceed without approval)

4. SPOT-CHECK PARENT MAPPINGS (Day 8-9)
   - Review sample parent_leaf_id mappings
   - Verify augmented images correctly map to originals
   - Confirm extraction logic is correct

5. VERIFY ZERO LEAKAGE (Day 12 - CRITICAL GATE)
   - Run verification script 3 times (all must pass)
   - Confirm zero parent overlap
   - Required before moving to Phase 2

6. PHASE 1 SIGN-OFF (Day 15 - CRITICAL GATE)
   - Verify all deliverables present
   - Confirm all gates passed
   - Approve Phase 1 as complete

CRITICAL PRINCIPLE:
Zero parent-image leakage - this is the entire foundation of Phase 1.
Use parent_leaf_id grouping (not file-level grouping).
Verify zero leakage 3 times before proceeding to Phase 2.

   first message.

Q: What are the critical gates?
A: Day 7 (freeze label ontology), Day 9 (verify manifest), Day 12 (verify
   zero leakage 3x), Day 15 (approve Phase 1 complete). All require your
   explicit approval.

Q: Can I skip any steps?
A: No. The foundation depends on all steps. Every step builds on the previous.

Q: What if I find issues?
A: Stop, document the issue, and ask Claude for help. The troubleshooting
   guides are included in the documents.

Q: How long will Phase 1 take?
A: 17 days (May 4-27, 2026) with 1-2 hours per day. Includes buffer days

A: 1,981 original + 20,019 augmented images of 7 medicinal plants from

Q: Can Claude automate the dataset download?

   manually.

Q: What if something breaks?
